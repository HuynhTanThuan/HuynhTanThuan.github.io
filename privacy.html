Privacy Policy

App: Sonic Pulse: Music Quiz Game
Developer: Huynh Tan Thuan
Effective date: April 2, 2026

1. Overview

Sonic Pulse is a music quiz game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

  • Anonymous user ID — generated automatically via Firebase Anonymous Authentication. No name, email, or personal information is required to play.
  • Username — a display name you choose yourself (no real name required).
  • Game data — scores, completion times, and answer history stored to power the leaderboard and your personal stats.
  • Device/usage data — collected by Firebase Analytics for crash reporting and app performance monitoring.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your stats and personal best on the leaderboard.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

Sonic Pulse uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, storage, and analytics.
  • Google AdMob — to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Your anonymous account and game data are retained as long as the app is in use. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Sonic Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
